# Sales analysis and dashboard built in Excel using Superstore dataset

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data from a Superstore dataset using Microsoft Excel.  
The goal is to understand sales performance, demand patterns, risk areas, and strategic priorities.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Online/Desktop)
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Dashboard Design
- Basic Excel formulas

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Year-over-Year Growth (2018 vs 2017)

## Analysis Sections
### 1. Impact (Top KPIs)
- Overall revenue growth of 20.3% compared to 2017
- Strong increase in total orders, indicating higher demand
- Slight dip in AOV, suggesting more frequent but smaller orders

### 2. Demand & Risk Analysis
- Phones, Chairs, and Storage show high demand
- Fasteners, Labels, and Supplies indicate low demand and potential risk

### 3. Strategic Prioritization
- West and East regions generate the highest revenue
- Consumer segment contributes the majority of sales

## Outcome
The dashboard helps identify high-performing areas, demand drivers, and risk-prone sub-categories to support business decision-making.
